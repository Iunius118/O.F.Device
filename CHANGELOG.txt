# Change Log
[O.F.Device](https://github.com/Iunius118/O.F.Device).

## [Unreleased]

## [1.1.0-beta.1]
### Added
- Add OF C Device, a cobblestone generator.

## [1.0.1]
### Fixed
- Recipe type for OF devices.

## [1.0.0]
### Changed
- Release.

## [1.0.0-beta.2]
### Added
- Six new looks to OF device blocks. Stick can be used on placed OF device blocks to change their appearance.

### Fixed
- Quick movement of item stack from player inventory to material slot.

## [1.0.0-beta.1]
### Added
- OF C Feeder I item. When this item is placed in the material slot of an OF device, the device is supplied with an unlimited supply of cobblestones. However, the fuel consumption is doubled.
- OF C Feeder II item. This item is the improved version of OF C Feeder I and does not increase fuel consumption.
- Crafting recipes for OF Device Mod 1 and Mod 2.
- Farming efficiency: appearance rate of ores increases with number of players, villagers, etc. (up to 3) around the device (5x3x5).
- Creative mode tab.
- Server-side config file: `(world_save_dir)/serverconfig/orefarmingdevice-server.toml`.

### Changed
- Require Forge 1.16.5-36.2.39+.
- Increase processing speed of OF Device Mod 1 and Mod 2.
- Increase fuel consumption of devices by modification.
- Version scheme.
- Bump version.

### Fixed
- Device blocks can now be removed with pickaxe.
- Item stack to move quickly to material slot.

## [1.16.5-0.1.0-Forge]
### Added
- OF Device block.
- OF Device Mod 1 block.
- OF Device Mod 2 block.
